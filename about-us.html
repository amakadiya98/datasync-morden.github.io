DataSync:
Cutting-Edge IT Innovation

As part of Elips Group Companies, DataSync operates with over 100 employees and 33+ years of experience in the IT industry, backed by strategic partnerships with industry leaders and a global customer portfolio.

The Story Behind DataSync:

Expertise Meets Innovation

DataSync was founded by a former Walmart Category Manager with a vision to expand brands in Turkey through online channels. However, the lack of advanced technology made scaling and managing operations a challenge.To bridge this gap, we teamed up with Elips Group, leveraging 33+ years of IT expertise to develop a powerful, AI-driven e-commerce and retail platform. By combining deep industry knowledge from Walmart with cutting-edge technology, DataSync was born—a solution designed to streamline operations, enhance scalability, and drive business growth.

The DataSync team now manages over $4 million annually through its platform, handling 50K+ SKUs across 15+ industries and 300+ brands—driving seamless operations, efficiency, and growth.

Mission

At DataSync, our mission is to empower businesses with AI-driven automation, data intelligence, and strategic consulting to optimize e-commerce, retail, and operational processes. We strive to eliminate inefficiencies, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making by providing a fully automated, all-in-one platform that seamlessly integrates financial, operational, marketing, and customer insights.

Vision

Our vision is to redefine the future of e-commerce and retail by becoming the leading AI-powered platform that bridges technology, automation, and expert consulting. We envision a world where businesses effortlessly manage operations, accelerate growth, and maximize profitability through intelligent, data-driven solutions—enabling them to stay ahead in the ever-evolving digital landscape.
